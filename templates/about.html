Professional Background

With both of these positions, I was able utilize some of my technical prowess to improve both of the businesses. I was able to integrate data analytics with the cattle, by registering them and exporting the data that we collect into excel spreadsheets, in order to determine the proper genetics that were needed to improve the future generations of the cattle. I was able to increase the concrete resurfacing company's exposure, by consulting with their current website support company on a new design and overall upgrade to their current website. I was also able to increase the productivity of the employees, by implementing the use of a mobile app that would track the employee's time working and using their gps, would show the locations in which the they clocked in or out.

Since I started, I have been in the top 5 for weekly sales on three seperate weeks within my first year. I was able to overcome my weaknesses and succeed. I was able to do this by utilizing my technical skills. I created my side project, protected-hollows-32305.herokuapp.com.
